# Valo Hub Privacy Policy

**Effective date: June 21, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how csaim ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Valo Hub Android application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using Valo Hub, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Where required by law, we ask for consent before processing information for personalized advertising or similar purposes.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide

Depending on how you use Valo Hub, you may provide:

- **Account information**, such as your email address and account identifier when you register or sign in.
- **User content**, such as gameplay clips, filenames, and information associated with an upload.
- **Communications**, including information you send when contacting us for support, privacy requests, or other questions.

Do not upload content containing sensitive personal information or personal information about another person unless you have the right to do so.

### Information collected automatically

Valo Hub and the service providers described below may automatically collect:

- Device and account identifiers, including an app-instance identifier, app-set ID, and Android advertising ID when available.
- IP address and approximate location inferred from the IP address.
- App activity and interactions, such as app launches, screens viewed, taps, ad impressions, ad interactions, and video views.
- Device and technical information, such as device model, operating-system version, app version, language, and network information.
- Diagnostic and performance information, such as app startup time, crashes, hangs, and energy usage.

The exact information collected may depend on your device settings, consent choices, region, and the features you use.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide, operate, maintain, and improve Valo Hub.
- Create and manage accounts and authenticate users.
- Receive, store, review, moderate, publish, or remove submitted gameplay clips.
- Deliver, measure, and improve advertising.
- Understand app usage and improve features, performance, and reliability.
- Detect fraud, abuse, security incidents, and violations of our rules.
- Respond to support requests and privacy requests.
- Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and users.

## 3. Advertising and Analytics

Valo Hub uses **Google AdMob** to display banner and interstitial advertisements. Depending on your region and privacy choices, Google may serve personalized, non-personalized, limited, or other legally permitted ads.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and share information including IP address, approximate location, app interactions, diagnostic information, advertising ID, app-set ID, and related device identifiers for advertising, analytics, measurement, and fraud-prevention purposes.

Valo Hub also uses **Google Analytics for Firebase** to understand how users interact with the app. Firebase Analytics may use app-instance and device identifiers and collect information about app activity and technical characteristics.

Where required, Valo Hub uses Google's User Messaging Platform to present privacy choices. Eligible users can accept, reject, or manage applicable advertising and data-processing choices. When required by the applicable message, users can revisit those choices through the app's privacy-options entry point.

Learn more:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
- Google advertising technologies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
- Firebase privacy and security: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## 4. How We Share Information

We may share or allow processing of information with:

- **Google Firebase**, for authentication, analytics, infrastructure, security, and related app services.
- **Google AdMob and participating advertising partners**, for advertising, measurement, reporting, and fraud prevention.
- **Backend and hosting providers**, including PythonAnywhere and other infrastructure providers used to host APIs, stream videos, and process uploads.
- **Professional advisers and authorities**, when reasonably necessary to comply with law, enforce agreements, protect rights and safety, or investigate fraud or abuse.
- **A successor organization**, in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of all or part of the Service.

We do not sell personal information for money. However, personalized advertising or disclosure to advertising partners may be considered "selling," "sharing," or "targeted advertising" under certain privacy laws. Where applicable, you can exercise opt-out rights through the privacy choices provided in the app or by contacting us.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including providing the Service, maintaining security, resolving disputes, and meeting legal obligations.

Account information may be retained while an account remains active. Uploaded clips and related metadata may be retained while they are published, awaiting moderation, or needed to enforce our rules. Advertising and analytics providers retain information according to their own policies and your applicable privacy choices.

We may retain limited records after deletion when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

## 6. Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

- Request access to or a copy of your personal information.
- Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
- Object to or restrict certain processing.
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
- Opt out of certain targeted advertising, sales, or sharing.
- Appeal a decision concerning a privacy request where applicable.
- Submit a complaint to an applicable data-protection authority.

You may also reset or delete your Android advertising ID through your device settings. Where required, advertising privacy choices can be managed through the privacy-options entry point in Valo Hub.

To submit a privacy or account-deletion request, contact us at **ravibramhadev.gavade@gwu.edu**. We may need to verify your request before completing it.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. Information transmitted by Google Mobile Ads and Firebase is encrypted in transit. No storage or transmission method is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Our service providers may process information in the United States and other countries. Those countries may have different data-protection laws from your country. Where required, service providers use recognized safeguards for international transfers.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Valo Hub is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through Valo Hub, contact us so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

## 10. Third-Party Services and Links

Valo Hub may contain advertisements, links, or integrations operated by third parties. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review their policies before providing information or interacting with their services.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to Valo Hub, our service providers, or applicable requirements. We will update the effective date above and provide additional notice when required by law.
